# Barakah Travels: Website Strategy & 90-Day Growth Plan

## 1. Your ideal customer (who the website speaks to)

**Primary:** Pakistani families (30-60) booking Umrah for themselves or their parents.
- **Pain points:** fear of hidden charges, agents disappearing after payment, hotels far
  from the Haram, elderly parents struggling without support.
- **Buying triggers:** Ramadan and school holidays approaching, a relative's
  recommendation, seeing a clear price with clear inclusions.
- **Objections:** "Is this agent genuine?", "What if the visa is refused?",
  "Will there be extra charges later?"
- **Desired outcome:** a peaceful, worry-free Umrah where everything simply works.

Every page of the site is built to answer these: prices are public and dated,
inclusions are listed, the comparison table tackles trust head-on, the FAQ answers
objections before they are asked, and every section ends at WhatsApp (the lowest-
friction contact channel in Pakistan).

## 2. What the site already has (conversion system)

- Hero with instant value proposition + dual CTA (packages / WhatsApp)
- Benefits grid, live-priced package cards, 4-step process
- **NEW: comparison table** (Barakah vs typical agents) for differentiation
- Testimonials, **NEW: objection-handling FAQ**, **NEW: urgency CTA band**
- About page rewritten around the customer's transformation
- Trust signals: prices dated ("Prices updated"), written-confirmation promise,
  24/7 WhatsApp, 570+ pilgrims served
- Technical SEO: titles, descriptions, TravelAgency schema, sitemap, Search Console

## 3. Priority audit (what to fix next, biggest impact first)

1. **Real testimonials.** Replace the placeholder quotes with real customer
   reviews (with permission), ideally with first name + city. Fake-looking proof
   hurts more than no proof. Screenshot WhatsApp thank-you messages work well.
2. **Real photos.** Add 3-5 photos of actual groups you have served (with faces
   blurred or permission). One real group photo beats ten stock images.
3. **Registration details.** If you have a company registration / IATA / Ministry
   of Hajj & Umrah authorisation number, put it in the footer. Biggest single
   trust jump available.
4. **Social links.** Footer Facebook/Instagram icons point to "#". Create the
   pages and link them, or remove the icons.
5. **Custom domain (~PKR 3,000/yr)** when budget allows: barakahtravels.pk.
   Better ranking, better trust, shorter WhatsApp shares.

## 4. 90-day action plan (highest ROI first)

**Weeks 1-2: Foundation**
- Upload this round of changes; confirm Search Console indexing of all 6 pages
- Complete Google Business Profile 100% (services, photos, description, hours)
- Ask your last 10 customers for a Google review (send them the direct link)

**Weeks 3-4: Proof**
- Collect 3 real testimonials and replace placeholders on the site
- Add real group photos to the Gallery
- Create Facebook + Instagram pages; post 2x/week (departure dates, tips, photos)

**Month 2: Reach**
- Post weekly "Umrah tip" content (visa documents checklist, what to pack,
  first-time pilgrim mistakes). Share to Facebook groups and WhatsApp status.
- Ask every travelling customer to share one photo + review
- Consider a small Google Ads test (PKR 500-1000/day) on "umrah package
  [your city]" keywords; WhatsApp click as the conversion goal

**Month 3: Convert & retain**
- Add a "Ramadan 2027 pre-booking" banner when dates are announced (urgency)
- Start a simple WhatsApp broadcast list of past enquiries: price updates,
  new departure dates (with their consent)
- Review Search Console: which queries show your site? Write one page/post
  targeting the top query you are not yet ranking well for

**Measure weekly:** WhatsApp enquiries (count them), Google Business Profile
views/calls, Search Console clicks. These three numbers tell you everything.
