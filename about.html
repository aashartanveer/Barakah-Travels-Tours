Who We Are

A Trusted Name in Umrah Travel

Barakah Travels is a dedicated Umrah travel agency committed to making the sacred journey accessible, comfortable and spiritually rewarding for every pilgrim. We understand that Umrah is one of life's most cherished experiences — and we treat it with the honour it deserves.

From visa and flights to hotels near the Haramain and on-ground support, our team manages every detail with care, transparency and the spirit of service.

  • Hundreds of satisfied pilgrims served
  • Honest, transparent and competitive pricing
  • Personal attention for families and groups
