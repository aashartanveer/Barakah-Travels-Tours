Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need for Umrah?

A passport valid for at least 6 months, passport-size photos, and a completed application. We guide you through the full list once you book.

Are the prices per person?

Yes, all quotes are per person and depend on travel season, room sharing (quad/triple/double) and hotel category. Message us for the current price.

Can I choose my own travel dates?

Absolutely. We offer fixed group departures as well as fully customized dates for families and private groups.

How close are the hotels to the Haram?

We prioritize hotels within easy walking distance of Masjid al-Haram and Masjid an-Nabawi. Exact distance depends on the package tier.

How do I confirm my booking?

Simply message us on WhatsApp. Our team will confirm availability, share the invoice, and guide you through payment and documentation.
